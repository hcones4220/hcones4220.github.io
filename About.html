Welcome to Arizona Airlines®,

We have been helping people travel between Arizona and its surrounding states since 1993. We are a privately owned company and only fly people to and from Arizona and its surrounding states, which include:

  • California
  • Nevada
  • Utah
  • Colorado
  • New Mexico

Even though we may seem like a smaller corporation that you may have never heard of, we guarantee top-of-the-line service for every traveler that chooses to fly with us. We take pride in the safety and security of ALL of our passengers and their belongings.
