Flight Privelages

Health Benefits

Employees can choose from medical coverage options ranging from PPOs to a high deductible health plan. We will pick up a generous amount of the cost, making the monthly amount Employees pay for medical and prescription drug coverage for themselves and their families shockingly affordable.

Wellness Rewards

Arizona Airlines® employees can live on and off the job with wellness checks and programs.

401(k) Plan

Save for your retirement through our 401(k) Plan with either pre-tax or Roth contributions. Arizona Arilines® will match employee contributions dollar for dollar up to 9% of their eligible earnings.

Working here will earn you technical experience as well as classroom knowledge that is particular to your field and available at some of the nation's top universities.

How to apply for a job

Here at Arizona Airlines® we try to make our application process as simple as possible.

  1. Check out all the information on this page when deciding to apply.
  2. Click below on your desired field of work.
  3. Read the job description.
  4. Fill out the form, attach your resume if you'd like, and hit the submit button to send your application to our corporate office for review.
  5. You will receive an email and a call if we would like you to come in for an interview.
  6. And you'll be on your way to having the best job of your life...
