Frequently Asked Questions

My schedule changed, can I change my flight reservation?

Yes, of course you can change your flight reservations. After you booked the flight, you will have received an email including all of the information for that flight, as well as a summary, and a couple of options. One of those options is REVIEW THIS FLIGHT RESERVATION, click on that link and it will take you to this website where you can sign in and review all flights or cars that you have booked. Select the flight you want to change and hit the EDIT button. Changing flight or car selections will not require any new payments or any more money unless you wish to refund you reservation.

I lost my Rewards Card, what do I do?

If you lose your Rewards Card or if it is stolen, do not worry. Give us a call and we are able to shut down the card so it cannot be used until it is back in your possession. We also have a tracking system that will alert us if the card is ever scanned in the United States.

I accidently left some luggage on the plane. Can I get it back?

Yes, you will be able to get your possessions back. After every flight, all luggage is taken off of the plane and available for pickup. The plane is then sweeped for everything that doesn't belong to us. If, for whatever reason, there is luggage left in our possession, it will be put in a secure storage facility for 60 days. At that time, if the items are not picked up, it will be donated away. If your give us a call or email regarding lost and found items we will tag that/those item(s) and get them back to you as soon as possible. Thank you.
